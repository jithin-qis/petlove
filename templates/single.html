Proper right way to treat your pets

This website provides complete treaqtment and care for your animal

The main aim of" PET SAKE " is to provide services to cattle farmers and pet lovers. It maintains the details of various veterinary doctors and livestock inspectors. It provides the facilities to appoint a doctor via online.

This app provides a detailed description of all animals like food, the diseases that they can affect, vaccination details, tips for how to take care of them etc.Chat bot is the other function that is helpful to users to post queries and doubts regarding anything. The users who installed this app will receive an insurance and vaccination notifications. So they can provide vaccination to their animals at the correct time and cattle farmers can apply insurance.

This is more user convenient as people from different locations can fix their appointment for consultant to their favorite doctors. Rescheduling/Cancelling of appointment is should available as in case of any urgency.
